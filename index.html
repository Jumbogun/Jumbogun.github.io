Research Interests

My current research interests mainly focused on Language Agents and Abductive Learning, with a focus on enabling Language Agents to automatically summarize and reuse knowledge. I aspire to develop agents that can autonomously abstract task-relevant concepts and formulate rules, much like humans do, and apply the acquired knowledge across various contexts.
